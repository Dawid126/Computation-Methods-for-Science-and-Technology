From the weekly Home Security report see TNA CAB 66/14/29.On the 7th January, during the most extensive daylight raiding that we have known for some weeks, London was raided intermittently for three and a half hours, and bombs were dropped in fifteen districts. On the same day many incidents were reported from East Anglia and the Home Counties, and one from Coventry. No important damage and few casualties were caused by these daylight raids. 7th december 1941 daylight raids around britain
