The important industrial center of Kharkov was captured by the Germans on the 24th October. The Soviets were now dismantling whole factories and shipping them east to resume production far away from the front line. It was a close run thing in Kharkov where the evacuation of a tank factory was completed with only a few days to spare. There was fierce fighting to protect the rail lines as the Germans came within seven miles of the railhead where the industrial equipment was loading.  24th october 1941 kharkov falls
