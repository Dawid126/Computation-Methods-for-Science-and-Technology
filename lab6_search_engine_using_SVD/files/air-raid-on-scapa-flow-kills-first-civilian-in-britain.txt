James Isbister, 27, an Orkney resident became the first British civilian to be killed in an air raid on March 16th 1940.  Fourteen Ju-88 Luftwaffe bombers attacked the British fleet at Scapa Flow and hit HMS Norfolk but some bombs hit cottages on the Mainland. The Royal Navy was making more use of Scapa Flow after the defences had been augmented following the sinking of the Royal Oak on [permalink id=356 text=’14th October 1939′].
